Skyrim, Tamriel

The frozen tundra of this breathtaking country may intimidate the average travler. But those brave enough to endure the harsh climate are rewarded with stunning mountainscapes and a fascinating variety of flora and funa.

The wildlife is exuberant and fascinating to behold, but only at a distance. While animals such as wolves, sabertooth tigers, and wooly mammoths can be breathtaking, they can also be exceedingly dangerous if provoked.

From Campfires to Cozy Inns

Skyrim offers many places to rest your head. Rough it in the wilderness with a warm bedroll and inviting champfire, or find comfort in any of the inns through out the holds. Here are some of the most suggested inns in the country:

  • The Winking Skeever-Solitude
  • The Bannered Mare-Whiterun
  • The Bee and Barb-Riften

Please Plan Your Trips Accordingly

Please remeber that while a beautiful vacation spot, Skyrim can be unforgiving terrain and is not for the faint of heart. Packing and preparing accordingly is keep to keeping safe in the winter cold. Here are some suggested items to carry along, just in case!

  1. Iron Sword and Hide Shield
  2. Warm Fur Armor
  3. A Thermal Bedroll-Even if you plan to sleep in an inn!
  4. Torch
  5. Extra Food and Water
